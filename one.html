Islands of Goa

Divar Island

Divar Island is a picturesque island located on the Mandovi river. The island is known for its scenic beauty, heritage houses, and the Our Lady of Compassion Church.

Chorao Island

Chorao Island is the largest island in Goa and is located on the Mandovi river. The island is home to the Dr. Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary, which is a must-visit for birdwatchers.

Grande Island

Grande Island, also known as Ilha Grande, is a popular spot for snorkeling and scuba diving. The island is located off the coast of Vasco da Gama and can be reached by boat.

Anjediva Island

Anjediva Island is a small island located off the coast of Karwar in Karnataka. The island is known for its historic Portuguese fort, which dates back to the 17th century.

Teracol Island

Teracol Island, also known as Terekhol Island, is located at the mouth of the Terekhol river. The island is home to the Terekhol Fort, which was built by the Portuguese in the 17th century.
