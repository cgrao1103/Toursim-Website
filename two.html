Churches of Goa

Basilica of Bom Jesus

The Basilica of Bom Jesus, located in Old Goa, is one of the most famous churches in Goa. It is known for its exquisite baroque architecture and the tomb of St. Francis Xavier, a revered Catholic saint.

Se Cathedral

The Se Cathedral, also located in Old Goa, is the largest church in Asia and one of the most impressive examples of Portuguese architecture in Goa. It was built to celebrate the victory of the Portuguese over the Muslim rulers of Goa in the 16th century.

Our Lady of Immaculate Conception

The Church of Our Lady of Immaculate Conception, located in Panaji, is one of the most recognizable landmarks of Goa. It is known for its striking white exterior and the bell tower that is visible from a distance.

St. Cajetan

The Church of St. Cajetan, located in Old Goa, is a stunning example of Corinthian architecture. It was modeled after the Basilica of St. Peter in Rome and is known for its elegant columns and intricate carvings.

Our Lady of the Mount

The Chapel of Our Lady of the Mount, located on top of a hill in Old Goa, offers breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape. It is a popular spot for tourists and locals alike and is known for its peaceful and serene atmosphere.
